⚖️ Balanced Trees

🎯 Why Balance?

Unbalanced BSTs degrade to O(n). Self-balancing trees maintain O(log n) by automatically rebalancing after insertion/deletion.

🌳 AVL Tree

  • Height difference (balance factor) of left and right subtrees ≤ 1
  • Rebalances via rotations (LL, RR, LR, RL rotations)
  • Strictly balanced → better read performance

🔴⚫ Red-Black Tree

  • Nodes are colored red or black
  • Guarantees O(log n) with fewer rotations than AVL
  • Used in Java's TreeMap and TreeSet

🎯 Summary

In practice, you rarely implement balanced trees from scratch — use Java's TreeMap / TreeSet (Red-Black internally).

🕸️ Graph Fundamentals

🎯 What is a Graph?

A graph is a set of vertices (nodes) connected by edges.

📋 Properties

  • Directed – Edges have direction (A→B)
  • Undirected – Edges are bidirectional (A—B)
  • Weighted – Edges have costs/distances
  • Cyclic vs Acyclic – Whether cycles exist

📌 Real-World Examples

  • Social networks: users are vertices, friendships are edges
  • Navigation: cities are vertices, roads are weighted edges
  • Web pages: pages are vertices, hyperlinks are directed edges

🎯 Summary

Graphs model relationships and networks. Understanding directed vs undirected, weighted vs unweighted is the starting point.

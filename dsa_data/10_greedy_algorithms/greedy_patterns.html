🟢 Greedy Patterns & Applications

📋 Common Greedy Patterns

  • Sort + select: Activity selection, fractional knapsack, job scheduling
  • Priority queue driven: Huffman, Prim's MST, Dijkstra, median of stream
  • Two-pass greedy: Candy problem (first left-to-right, then right-to-left)
  • Interval greedy: Minimum number of arrows to burst balloons, non-overlapping intervals

🎯 Interview Tips

  • Try greedy first — if it gives wrong answers for edge cases, switch to DP
  • Always verify with a counter-example before trusting a greedy approach
  • Common wrong greedy: Coin change with non-canonical coins, 0/1 knapsack (greedy fails here)

🎯 Summary

Greedy algorithms are fast and elegant. Learn the key problems where greedy is provably optimal: interval scheduling, MST, Huffman, fractional knapsack.

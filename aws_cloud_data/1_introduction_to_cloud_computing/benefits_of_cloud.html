✅ Benefits of Cloud Computing

💡 Six Key Benefits (AWS Perspective)

  1. Trade capital expense for variable expense – Pay only for what you use
  2. Massive economies of scale – Millions of customers → lower costs shared across all
  3. Stop guessing capacity – Scale instantly; no over-provisioning or under-provisioning
  4. Increase speed and agility – New resources in minutes, not weeks
  5. Stop spending money running data centers – Focus on your business, not infrastructure
  6. Go global in minutes – Deploy in any AWS region instantly

📋 Operational Benefits

  • High availability: Multiple AZs and Regions for redundancy
  • Elasticity: Auto Scaling adjusts capacity automatically
  • Disaster recovery: Automated backups, multi-region replication

🎯 Summary

Cloud eliminates heavy lifting: no hardware, no data center maintenance, no capacity guessing — just build and ship faster.

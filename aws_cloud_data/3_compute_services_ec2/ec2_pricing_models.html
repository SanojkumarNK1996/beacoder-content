💲 EC2 Pricing Models

💡 On-Demand Instances

Pay for compute capacity by the second (Linux) or hour (Windows). No commitment. Best for: short-term workloads, unpredictable traffic, development and testing.

📅 Reserved Instances

Commit to 1 or 3 years for up to 72% cost savings. Best for: steady-state workloads (production databases, app servers).

✋ Spot Instances

Bid for unused EC2 capacity at up to 90% discount. AWS can reclaim with 2-minute warning. Best for: fault-tolerant batch jobs, ML training, big data processing.

🏠 Dedicated Hosts

Physical server dedicated to you. Required for compliance (bring-your-own-license, regulatory requirements). Most expensive option.

💰 Savings Plans

Flexible pricing (like Reserved but applies to any instance family/region) with 1 or 3-year commitment.

🎯 Summary

On-Demand = flexibility, Reserved = savings for predictable workloads, Spot = maximum savings for tolerant jobs. Mix and match for optimal cost.

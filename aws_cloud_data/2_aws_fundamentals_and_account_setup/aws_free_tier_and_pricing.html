💰 AWS Free Tier and Pricing

🎁 AWS Free Tier

AWS offers a Free Tier for 12 months after account creation (and some services are always free). Key free-tier limits:

  • EC2: 750 hours/month of t2.micro or t3.micro
  • S3: 5 GB storage
  • RDS: 750 hours of db.t2.micro/db.t3.micro
  • Lambda: 1 million requests/month (always free)
  • DynamoDB: 25 GB storage (always free)

💳 AWS Pricing Principles

  • Pay for what you use: Compute billed per second/hour
  • Pay less when you reserve: 1 or 3-year Reserved Instances save up to 75%
  • Pay less with volume: Tiered pricing as usage grows

Internet Basics

The internet is a global network that connects computers and allows people to share information.

Key Concepts

  • Browsers: Programs like Chrome, Firefox, and Safari that let you view websites.
  • Websites: Collections of web pages that provide information, services, or applications.
  • Search Engines: Tools like Google and Bing that help you find information online.

Internet Safety

  • Use strong passwords and update them regularly.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading files from unknown sources.
  • Keep software and antivirus programs up to date.

Types of Computers

Computers come in many forms, each designed for specific tasks and environments.

Desktop Computers

Desktop computers are personal computers designed for regular use at a single location. They are powerful, upgradable, and commonly used in homes and offices.

Laptops

Laptops are portable personal computers that integrate the display, keyboard, and battery in one unit. They are convenient for users who need mobility.

Servers

Servers provide services and resources to other computers over a network. They are used to host websites, applications, files, and databases.

Supercomputers

Supercomputers are highly powerful machines built for complex calculations, scientific research, weather forecasting, and large-scale simulations.

Embedded Systems

Embedded systems are computers built into devices like microwaves, cars, medical equipment, and smart appliances. They perform specific functions.

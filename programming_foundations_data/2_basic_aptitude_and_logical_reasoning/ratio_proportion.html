Ratio & Proportion

Ratio compares two quantities while proportion shows that two ratios are equal.

Ratio

Written as a:b or a/b. Example: 4 apples to 2 oranges = 4:2 = 2:1.

Proportion

Example: 2/3 = 4/6. If one ratio equals another, they are in proportion.

Applications

  • Mixing ingredients
  • Scale models
  • Sharing quantities

Profit & Loss

Profit and loss measure how much money is gained or lost when buying and selling goods.

Key Terms

  • Cost Price (CP): Amount paid to buy an item.
  • Selling Price (SP): Amount received from selling an item.
  • Profit: SP > CP
  • Loss: CP > SP

Formulas

  • Profit = SP - CP
  • Loss = CP - SP
  • Profit % = (Profit / CP) × 100
  • Loss % = (Loss / CP) × 100

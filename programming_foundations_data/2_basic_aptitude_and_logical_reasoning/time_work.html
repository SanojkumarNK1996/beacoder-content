Time & Work

Time and work problems calculate how long it takes for people or machines to complete tasks.

Basic Concept

Work = Rate × Time. If one person can finish a job in 5 hours, their rate is 1/5 of the job per hour.

Examples

  • If A takes 4 hours and B takes 6 hours, together they do 1/4 + 1/6 = 5/12 of the work per hour.
  • Then combined time = 1 / (5/12) = 12/5 = 2.4 hours.

Percentages

Percentages express a number as a fraction of 100. They are widely used in finance, data analysis, and everyday comparisons.

Formula

Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

Examples

  • 50 out of 200 = (50 / 200) × 100 = 25%
  • 20% of 150 = 0.20 × 150 = 30

Applications

  • Discounts and sales
  • Interest rates
  • Grades and statistics
